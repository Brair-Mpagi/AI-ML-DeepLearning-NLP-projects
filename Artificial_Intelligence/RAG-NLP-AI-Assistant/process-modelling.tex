\subsubsection{Process Modelling}

Process modelling captures how the system behaves at runtime, illustrating
the flow of interactions between users, system components, and data stores
\cite{reddy2025design}.

\textbf{Use Case Diagram}

Figure~\ref{fig:use_case} presents the use case diagram illustrating the
interactions between system actors and the chatbot's primary functions. The
two primary actors are the \textbf{Stakeholder} (students, staff, prospective
students) and the \textbf{Administrator}.
